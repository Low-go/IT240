The Longship


Information about myself


"A ship is safe at harbor, but that's not what ships are for"
William Shepp

Ahoy! I'm a Viking and my favorite boat is the longboat. It's a versatile and sturdy vessel that's perfect for transportation and war. It can hold up to 80 rowers, allowing us to travel long distances quickly. I love its speed and maneuverability, making it perfect for surprise attacks and coastal raids. The long and narrow design allows it to cut through water with ease. And it's highly customizable, with the ability to add shields, dragonheads, and other decorations that make it a fearsome sight to behold on the open seas.
